OUR BRAND HISTORY

The RAINYDAYS raincoat selling store started in the 1990s as a small family-run business. My father John, a trained tailor, launched the store in a small town named Lillestrøm.He focused in producing and selling raincoats for both men and women. At the beginning, the shop was slow going.He had to fight back with other regional stores, and the raincoat market wasn’t as popular then as it is now.

But his passion to quality, artistry and skills soon paid off. Name of his store spread, and RAINYDAYS began to attract customers from all over the region. Soon RAINYDAYS launched additional stores across Norway. In addition to selling traditional raincoats, the RAINYDAYS also selling stylish raincoats with a range of colors and designs. RAINYDAYS were always looking for ways to develop and stay ahead of the competition. In the late 1999s, RAINYDAYS began selling raincoats made from recycled materials.

This was an innovative concept at the time and assisted RAINYDAYS to attract new generation customers who were more conscious of nvironmental issues. Over the years, RAINYDAYS has expanded its offerings to include a wide range of rainwear, such as rain jackets, capes, umbrellas and waterproof boots, to it’s collection. You will never go wrong wearing our product. We are concern of environment and sustainability. All of our rain jackets are waterproof and windproof.
